# 📊 Coca-Cola US Sales & Profitability Executive Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
An enterprise-grade Business Intelligence dashboard engineered using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze and visualize the regional sales dynamics, operational profitability channels, and brand-level performance metrics of **The Coca-Cola Company** across various United States markets. 

This interactive system transforms raw commercial records into a high-fidelity data asset, empowering executive stakeholders to drive strategic growth and maximize operating efficiency.

---

## 🛠️ Data Engineering & ETL Pipeline (Power Query)
Before initializing the design, a robust data sanitation and preprocessing pipeline was executed inside the **Power Query Engine** to meet corporate data quality standards:
1. **Structural Cleansing:** Removed redundant top-header anomalies and blank rows generated by spreadsheet merge formatting.
2. **Feature Pruning:** Inspected all attributes for structural missing values and purged empty columns to protect system performance and file density.
3. **Data Type Optimization:** Converted financial parameters into precise Currency units (`$`), temporal markers into standardized Date formats, and operating metrics into Percentages (`%`).
4. **Localization & Currency Ingestion:** Implemented customized formatting strings (`"$" #,0`) to apply universal dollar denotations without system overhead.

---

## 🎨 Interactive Visual Architecture & UI/UX Features
The interface implements an executive dark-ribbon layout designed for optimal visual contrast and high scannability:
* **Executive KPI Ribbon:** Displays real-time primary business anchors including **Total Sales ($8.68M)**, **Operating Profit ($3.17M)**, and a **36% Average Operating Margin**.
* **Dual-Axis Temporal Tracking (Line Chart):** Dynamically plots a dual-line time-series trend connecting monthly gross revenues against net operational profits from January to December.
* **Geospatial Intelligence Mapping:** Integrates native GIS plotting, scaling custom marker densities across US geographic clusters to instantaneously reveal revenue epicenters.
* **Corporate Brand Matrix:** Implements an analytical grid detailing units sold, gross revenues, and net margins segmented across target product lines (Coca-Cola, Diet Coke, Sprite, Dasani).
* **Responsive Cross-Filtering UI:** Configured modern, low-footprint button slicers (`Retailers` & `Regions`) ensuring immediate cross-graph recalculation upon click events.
